COVID-19 AND THE FUTURE OF SITE MONITORING

In response to the Covid-19 outbreak Visualskies are mobilising their full range of aerial technology and 3D expertise to help circumvent some of the issues that construction industry professionals are currently experiencing. Drone technology enables us to remotely access sites whilst, at the same time, maintaining the social distancing that is so essential to our welfare at the moment.

Our services to support you in this time

FREE* LIVE STREAMING

We’re utilising our aerial fleet to promote social distancing. To do our part we’re offering free aerial remote live viewing to help ease the burden on on-site staff, allowing site owners and stakeholders to assess their site remotely without the need to send staff into an area where the infection could potentially be spread. If you have a concern regarding your site then feel free to get in touch. We also offer a range of monitoring options to complement this service.

SURVEILLANCE

Aerial video/ photography can be utilised to view parts of a site which cannot be accessed by a static camera. This data capture method forms the basis of all Visualskies site monitoring services. It can be viewed either via live streaming to a smartphone or tablet, saved as a record of evidence to return to later or used to extract further data.

AERIAL BIM SCANNING

By utilising the latest UAV-bourne technologies including aerial LiDAR and photogrammetry, we’re able to capture large scale data assets in a manner not previously possible. Whether scanned at the project outset as the basis to model from or during the programme to use as an analysis tool, aerial scanning is fast becoming a requirement on major Building Information Modelling (BIM) projects across the UK.
